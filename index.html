Registration is open for the Portland Online 18xx Convention

What

Join us for three full days of online 18xx gaming. And this year, you can do it from the comfort of your own home (or anywhere you can get an internet connection).

When

June 25th
Pre Con warm up and familiarization with Discord/Online gaming platforms
June 26th-28th
Convention Gaming starting at 8:30am PDT/UTC -7 each day.

How

The general format will be two games of live simultaneous play per day using a Discord Channel. There will be a meeting room for game organization and announcements and game breakout rooms for live audio communication and screen sharing as needed.

The current list of games available to play is available in an online spreadsheet. This is a work in progress which will be updated as we learn more.

We will be utilizing these online gaming platforms (and possibly a couple more – stay tuned):

  • Board 18
  • TableTop Simulator
  • 18xx.games
  • RR18xx
  • Rails

To register complete this online registration form and we’ll be in touch with more information.

Hope to “see” you all there!
