Registrations are open for the 21st Portland 18xx con.

Its become a tradition. Experience 4 days of 18xx gaming in a fun and friendly environment. Bring a favorite or choose from the large collection of games we and others bring. Most are able to play at least 2 or 3 games a day. Several nearby choices for meals including the hotel.

This is a great place to test your skills against many of the best players in the hobby, get out of the group think of your home group and learn a new strategy or two.

Rooms are available from $134 + tax and can be reserved here!
